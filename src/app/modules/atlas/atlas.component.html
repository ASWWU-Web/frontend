About Us

Our focus is crafting some of the highest quality coffee and tea beverages available in the Walla Walla Valley while maintaining a rustic, community based atmosphere. All are invited to read, chat, collaborate, or simply enjoy the space; perfect for a long haul study session or a quick drink with a friend.
